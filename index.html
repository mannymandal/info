ABOUT.
Manish is a Bengali boy, born and raised in Chennai. He is at present, pursuing Masters in Geoinformatics after completing his Bachelors in Engineering. Contrary to his background, Manish's interests spans a diverse range of subjects. He is a budding writer and an infopreneur. He loves to talk to and motivate people and aspires to become a lifecoach one day. He is a vivid speaker, self-learner, astrosexual and a preacher of stoicism. When no one's around, Manish loves to chill with a fresh batch of podcasts, streaming music and stargazing.
